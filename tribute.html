Sir Timothy Berners-Lee

the man who invented the world wide web (www)

here is what we need to know about Timothy Berners-Lee

  1. Sir Timothy Berners-Lee was born in the year 1955 in London, England.
  2. He's the eldest of the four children born to Mary Lee Woods and Conway Berners-Lee.
  3. His parents were computer scientist who worked for the first commercially built computer the feranti mark 1.
  4. He attended Sheen Mount Primary School and then went on to attend South West London's Emmanuel school from 1969 to 1973.
  5. He worked as an Independent contractor at CERN from June to December 1980
  6. While in Geneva, he proposed a project based on hypertext, to facilitate the sharing and updating of information among researcher.
  7. After leaving CERN in 1980, he went to work at John Poole's Image Computer systems.
  8. In 1984 he returned back to CERN
  9. In 1989 CERN was the largest Internet node in Europe, and Berners-Lee saw the opportunity to join hypertext with the Internet.
  10. Berners-Lee wrote his proposal in March 1989 and in 1990 redistributed it.
  11. in 1994 he founded W3Cin Massachusetts Institute of Technology.
  12. In November 2009 Berners-Lee launched WWWF inorder to compaign toadvance the Web to empower humanity by launching transformative programs that build local capacity to leverage the Web as a medium for positive change.
  13. Berners-Lee is one of the pioneer vioces in favour of net neutrality, and has expressed th view that ISPs should supply connectivity without any strings attached to it, and should neither control or monitor the browsinf activities of customers without their expressed consent.
